Known as the "City of Eternal Spring," Medellin boasts a pleasant climate year-round, with temperatures that remain mild and comfortable. Medellin has undergone a remarkable transformation in recent years, shedding its turbulent past and emerging as a thriving hub of innovation and progress.

Fun Fact: Medellin is home to the annual Flower Festival, known as Feria de las Flores, which is one of the city's most iconic and beloved events. During this vibrant festival, the streets of Medellin come alive with colorful parades, elaborate flower displays, and lively music and dance performances. The highlight of the festival is the Desfile de Silleteros, a parade where local farmers carry intricately designed flower arrangements on their backs, showcasing the region's rich floral heritage. This celebration of flowers and nature is a testament to Medellin's commitment to preserving its natural beauty and promoting cultural traditions.
